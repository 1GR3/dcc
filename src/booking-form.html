Damage deposit

Dorset Coastal Cottages require a £150 security deposit for your booking.

This deposit is applied to:

  • All cottages with 4 or more bedrooms
  • All bookings made by non-UK residents
  • Other bookings where the deposit has been applied at the company's discretion

You will be contacted to provide card details to pay the Damage Deposit 7 days prior to your arrival. The amount will then be credited back to the same card 7 working days after the date of departure.
