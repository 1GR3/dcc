Property Description

Located on an exclusive estate, this 19th Century former kennel for working dogs, is Grade II Star listed and is located within the owner's gravelled courtyard. It offers spacious accommodation with a high level of comfort in peaceful and tranquil surroundings. All on one level with a high to low sloping ceiling running the length of the building. The secluded garden is reached via a gate across the courtyard and is laid to lawn with terraced area, original flint wall, trellis, shrubs and climbers. Parking for 1 car.

Shop 4 miles. 2 Hotels 0.75 miles & 3 miles. All on one level with oak floors throughout. From Entrance door step up to the beamed, open plan Kitchen/Sitting/Dining Room with well defined areas, oak worktops, dishwasher, space for coats and boots (no washing machine); Hall with door to courtyard; beamed Double Bedroom; Shower Bathroom.

Central heating. No telephone. Wireless Broadband (email and web browsing only). Rousdon Estate has a private beach for guests to use, Click Here to view a detailed description of how to access the beach. Please see our help and information page for standard facilities found in all our cottages.

Short breaks

Short breaks are welcomed at this property all year round, but is subject to peak time restrictions*. For a 3 night short break save 30% off a week's price. Mon–Fri breaks are offered at 4 nights for the price of 3. (4 nights are priced at 76.7%, 5 at 85% and 6 at 95% of the weeks price)

*A short break during peak times may only be booked within 2 weeks of the required dates. Peak times are: June to September, October and end of May half terms, Christmas, New Year and Easter. For more information please visit our Dorset Short Breaks Information Page.
